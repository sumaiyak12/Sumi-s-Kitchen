Seekh Kebab Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500 g minced meat (chicken, lamb, or beef – finely ground)
  • 1 medium onion (finely chopped)
  • 2–3 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp fresh coriander leaves (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • ½ tsp red chili powder
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp gram flour (roasted, for binding)
  • 2 tbsp oil or melted butter (for basting)
  • Skewers (wooden or metal)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the mixture: In a bowl, combine minced meat, onion, chilies, coriander, ginger-garlic paste, and all spices. Add gram flour and lemon juice. Mix well and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  2. Shape the kebabs: Grease hands with oil, take a portion of the mixture, and wrap it around a skewer to form long kebabs.
  3. Cook the kebabs:
    • On Grill/Pan: Heat a grill pan or tawa, brush with oil, and cook kebabs until browned.
    • In Oven: Preheat oven to 200°C (390°F), bake kebabs for 15–20 minutes, basting with oil/butter.
  4. Serve: Garnish with lemon wedges and onion rings. Serve hot with mint chutney or yogurt dip.
